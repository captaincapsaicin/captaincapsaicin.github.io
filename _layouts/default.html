Neil Thomas

I work at the intersection of AI and biology. I was a Research Scientist at Google X.

I completed my PhD at the UC Berkeley in 2022, advised by Yun S. Song. Prior to that, I was an AI Resident at Google X and a Software Engineer at 23andMe. I received my BS in Engineering Mathematics and Statistics from UC Berkeley.

I like cooking Alison Roman recipes, comedy, mountains, and ultimate frisbee.

Research

My research focuses on learning meaningful representations of proteins, with the aim of enabling applications in protein design, functional annotation, and structure prediction. My thesis talk "Browsing in the Library of Babel" serves as an accessible introduction.
